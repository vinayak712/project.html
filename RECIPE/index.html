Gulab Jamun

  • Sugar
  • Dough
  • Milk
  • oil

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, ghee, and milk to form a soft dough; shape into small balls
  2. Deep-fry the balls in ghee on low heat until golden brown.
  3. Prepare a sugar syrup by boiling water, sugar, and cardamom, then soak the fried balls in warm syrup for at least 30 minutes before serving

Berfie

  • Sugar
  • Milk Powder
  • ghee
  • cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan, add milk and milk powder, stirring until smooth and lump-free.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom powder, cooking on low heat until the mixture thickens and starts leaving the pan's sides.
  3. Spread the mixture onto a greased plate, garnish with nuts or saffron, let it cool, then cut into squares to serve
